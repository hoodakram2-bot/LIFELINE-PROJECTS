Who We Are

About LifeLine

LifeLine was founded in 2026 by a team of medical professionals and technologists who witnessed firsthand the devastating consequences of blood shortages in Ugandan hospitals. Our mission is to eliminate preventable deaths caused by the inability to find compatible blood donors in time.
